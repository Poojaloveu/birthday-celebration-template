Please add 6 photos to this folder with the following names:

- `photo1.jpg`
- `photo2.jpg`
- `photo3.jpg`

- `photo6.jpg`

## Tips:

- Use high-quality images (recommended size: at least 800x800 pixels)
- Photos will be displayed as square thumbnails
- Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
